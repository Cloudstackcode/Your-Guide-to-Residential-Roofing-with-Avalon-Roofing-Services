How to Know It’s Time for a New Roof

Many homeowners aren’t sure when their roof needs attention. Here are some common signs that it’s time to give us a call:

  • Missing, cracked, or curling shingles
  • Water stains on ceilings or walls
  • Granules in gutters or downspouts
  • Sagging areas or visible dips
  • Light shining through the attic
  • Roof is over 20 years old

If you’re noticing any of these issues—or just have a hunch something’s not right—we’re happy to take a look. Sometimes a small repair is all that’s needed. Other times, a full replacement may be more cost-effective in the long run. Either way, we’ll give you honest advice based on our years of residential roofing experience.
